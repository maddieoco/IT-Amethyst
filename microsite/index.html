About the Designer

hello world! I'm maddie and I love color. I'm constantly curious and this curiosity has taken me all sorts of places-an art degree, working at a web agency, then an animal shelter, and constantly adventuring in the world with a camera in my hand. while my degree is in fiber arts and textiles, and fabric will always be my first love, i've come to cherish the digital world and the way it shapes our lives. design, too is part of this ! I want in on that. so this assignment is shameless self-promotion.
