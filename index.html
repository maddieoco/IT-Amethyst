About Me

hello world! I'm maddie and I love color. I'm constantly curious and this curiosity has taken me all sorts of places-an art degree, working at a web agency, then an animal shelter, and constantly adventuring in the world with a camera in my hand.

while my degree is in fiber arts and textiles, and fabric will always be my first love, i've come to cherish the digital world and the way it shapes our lives. design, too is part of this ! I want in on that. so this page is shameless self-promotion.

what's the point?

If content is really what makes a good website, then this is an attempt at a more minimal, type-focused design.

I've seen too many artist websites that are either so minimal they're soulless or so busy they're incomprehensible. This is an attempt to find a middle ground.
